Host City

Gold Coast is a coastal city in Queensland, Australia, situated south of Brisbane along the country's eastern shoreline. Renowned for its pristine beaches, world-class surf breaks, and vibrant nightlife, the city also features a range of major theme parks, including Dreamworld, Sea World, and Warner Bros. Movie World. As a leading tourist destination, the Gold Coast attracts visitors with its extensive shopping precincts, diverse entertainment offerings, and rich natural landscapes. The region is surrounded by subtropical rainforests, scenic mountain ranges, and several national parks, contributing to its appeal as both an urban and ecological destination.
