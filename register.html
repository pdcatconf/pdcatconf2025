Registration

PDCAT 2025 registration is open now. Please click on the link to register for the conference: REGISTER NOW

At least one author of each accepted paper must register to ensure that the paper will be included in the PDCAT 2025 conference proceedings. Note that each accepted paper must have at least one full registration, either Early Bird or Late Full Registration.

If you have any special dietary requirements for the conference dinner, please email pdcatconf@gmail.com by 10 Nov 2025.

Registration Fee


Registration Type Date Registration Fee
Early Bird Full Registration by 23:59 Sep 30, 2025 (AoE) AUD 900
Late Full Registration after 23:59 Sep 30, 2025 (AoE) AUD 1100
Industry Workshop Registration (half-day) by 23:59 Nov 22, 2025 (AoE) AUD 300

Terms and Conditions:

  1. Full registration includes: Access to all PDCAT Workshops and electronic download of the workshop proceedings, catering during conference, and conference banquet.
  2. Industry workshop registration includes the afternoon session on and conference banquet on 23rd Nov.
  3. Cancellation, Refund and No-show Policies
    • No refund for paper registration.
    • Refund is possible ONLY for non-paper-affiliated registrations.
    • No refund would be issued unless a cancellation request with your affiliation stamp is sent to the conference local arrangement chair by 15 Oct, 2025 (AoE).
    • Refunds will be processed after the conference concludes.

Please contact Dr Yanming Zhu (yanming.zhu@griffith.edu.au) if you have any questions.

Travel VISA

For visa application purpose, please contact Dr Yanming Zhu (yanming.zhu@griffith.edu.au) to request an official invitation letter to PDCAT2025 after you have registered online. Please provide your name and affiliation, paper title and author list, as well as the registration payment confirmation to request the invitation letter.
